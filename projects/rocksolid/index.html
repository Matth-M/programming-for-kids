Welcome to the December 2023 Rock Solid Challenge!

The objective is to construct a device that not only protects the egg during a fall but also ensures its rapid descent. The winner will be determined by whose egg descends the quickest without breaking.


Rules for December 2023:

  • Objective: Create a device to safely drop an egg from a set height quickly. Egg must remain intact.
  • Materials: Limited to $5 budget. Keep receipts for verification.
  • Drop Height: From the top window of the organizing parent's house. The contraption can be thrown with force.
  • Timing: Measured with a 240 fps iPhone 8+ camera for accuracy. The time is taken from the moment it leaves the thrower's hand, until the contraption touches the ground.
  • Inspection: All devices inspected for compliance before the drop.
  • Eggs: Provided on-site. Random selection from a fresh carton.
  • Reusable Design: Must work for two drops without part replacement. Longer drop time counts.
  • Size and Weight: The contraption must encase the egg and must not exceed a weight of 1 kilogram.
  • Safety: Ensuring safety is paramount, both for those launching the contraption and for bystanders observing or passing by. The organizing parent holds responsibility for maintaining a safe environment.
  • Scoring: Fastest intact egg drop wins.
  • Prize: Winner gets a $20 prize.
