Welcome to the Rock Solid Challenge!

Here are the rules for the December 2023 engineering challenge:

  • Objective: Design a device to safely transport an egg from a specified height to the ground as quickly as possible. The egg must remain whole.
  • Materials: Use materials within a $5 budget. Receipts may be required for verification.
  • Height: The egg will be dropped from a predetermined height, the same for all participants. (the top window of the house of the parent organizing)
  • Time Measurement: The time taken for the egg to reach the ground will be recorded with slow motion iPhone 8+ camera, it records at 240 fps, so an accurate measurement can be made.
  • Inspection: Devices will be inspected for rule adherence before the drop.
  • Budget Limit: Participants must construct their device within a $5 budget.
  • Prize: The winner receives a $20 prize.
  • Scoring: The winner is determined by the shortest time for the egg to reach the ground without breaking.

NB: The parent organizing the challenge must pay the prize, but the kids participating must pay for their 5$ budget from their lunch money.
